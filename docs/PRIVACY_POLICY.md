# Privacy Policy for Buddy - AI Learning Companion

**Last updated:** December 1, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Buddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:
- Create an account (name, email, grade level)
- Complete your profile (subjects of interest, learning preferences)
- Use the app features (learning history, assessment results)
- Communicate with us (support requests, feedback)

### Learning Data
- Study sessions and time spent
- Questions asked and concepts learned
- Assessment scores and quiz results
- Progress tracking and achievement data
- XP points and badges earned
- Subject-wise performance metrics

### Usage Information
- App features used
- Navigation patterns
- Device information (type, OS version)
- App version and settings
- Error logs for debugging

### Parent Portal Data
- Parent-child relationships
- Goals and rewards set by parents
- Comments and notes from parents
- Weekly progress reports

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
1. **Provide Learning Services**
   - Deliver personalized learning experiences
   - Generate AI-powered explanations and guidance
   - Track and display progress
   - Create adaptive learning paths

2. **Improve Our Services**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Identify and fix technical issues
   - Develop new features
   - Enhance AI tutor accuracy

3. **Communication**
   - Send learning reminders
   - Notify about achievements
   - Share weekly progress reports with parents
   - Respond to support requests

4. **Safety and Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Ensure platform security
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Protect user rights

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing
We only share your information in these specific cases:

1. **Parent Portal** - With parents who have been authorized by the student through invitation codes
2. **Service Providers** - With trusted service providers who help us operate the app (hosting, analytics, AI services) under strict confidentiality agreements
3. **Legal Requirements** - When required by law or to protect rights and safety
4. **With Your Consent** - When you explicitly authorize sharing

## Data Security

### Our Security Measures
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls:** Strict access limits with multi-factor authentication
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Industry-standard cloud hosting with redundancy
- **Data Backup:** Regular automated backups with secure storage

### Your Security
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Don't share your login credentials
- Enable parent portal only with trusted adults
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** After 12 months of inactivity, we may delete your account
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data** - Request a copy of your personal information
2. **Correct Your Data** - Update incorrect or incomplete information
3. **Delete Your Data** - Request account and data deletion
4. **Export Your Data** - Download your learning history and progress
5. **Opt-Out** - Disable non-essential data collection
6. **Control Parent Access** - Revoke parent portal access anytime

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- **In-App:** Use Settings > Privacy & Data
- **Email:** privacy@buddylearning.com
- **Response Time:** We respond within 30 days
